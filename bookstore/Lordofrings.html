Book Description

The Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien is an epic high fantasy trilogy that follows a humble hobbit, Frodo Baggins, who inherits a powerful and evil ring forged by the dark lord Sauron. To save Middle-earth, Frodo and a fellowship of allies embark on a perilous journey to destroy the Ring in the fires of Mount Doom, facing war, temptation, and sacrifice along the way.
