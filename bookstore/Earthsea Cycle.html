Book Description

The Earthsea Cycle by Ursula K. Le Guin is a landmark fantasy series set in a vast archipelago where magic, dragons, and ancient powers shape the lives of its people. The story begins with Ged, a gifted young wizard whose pride unleashes a dangerous shadow into the world. His journey to confront this shadow becomes a powerful metaphor for self-discovery and balance. Later books shift focus to other characters like Tenar and King Lebannen, exploring deeper themes of identity, gender, and mortality
