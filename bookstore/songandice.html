Book Description

A Song of Ice and Fire by George R.R. Martin is an epic fantasy series set in a richly detailed world, primarily on the continents of Westeros and Essos. The story unfolds through multiple perspectives, following a brutal struggle for power among noble families after the death of King Robert Baratheon—the War of the Five Kings—while a greater, existential threat looms from the supernatural Others beyond the Wall in the frozen North. Simultaneously, Daenerys Targaryen, exiled daughter of a deposed king, rises from obscurity in the East, claiming dragons and building an army to reclaim the Iron Throne.

What You Will Learn

  • Power corrupts and destroys
  • Honor has consequences
  • Perspective shapes truth
  • Survival requires adaptability
  • Winter is coming—prepare for the long term
